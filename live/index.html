Map

The following is the layout of HackOHI/O in the Ohio Union. The Grand Ballroom and Great Hall are the designated hacking areas. The Showcase (Sunday, 2:30 PM) will be held in the Performance hall. In addition, there are several other spaces around the Union where you can find tech talks, relaxation retreats, and gaming.



Schedule

The event will take place in the Ohio Union (1739 North High Street). Please be aware that no one will be permitted to enter or exit the building from 11:30 PM to 7:00 AM on Sunday, November 20th.



Challenges

Several OHI/O sponsors have provided you with challenges you can incorporate into your project. The best projects for each challenge will win prizes.
