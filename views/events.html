OHI/O Sponsored Events

In anticiaption for OHI/O 2015, student organizers will collaborate with fellow tech student orgs to host tech talks, workshops, and social events throughout the semesters.

Open Source Club

Thursdays at 7:00 PM, Caldwell 120

The Open Source Club at the Ohio State University is a student organization composed of members who share a common enthusiasm for free, open source software. Our focus is on building a strong community of open source users and developers in order to bring the benefits the respective communities.

Mobile App Club

Tuesdays at 6:30 PM, Caldwell 120

The Mobile App Club at OSU was designed by students who are passionate about mobile devices. Our goal is to ship mobile applications using a business approach to prepare future mobile developers for the real world. Students will pick up Objective-C, Java, and Git.

CWDG

Wednesdays at 7:00 PM, Dreese 260

Collegiate Web Developers are a collection of students, graduates and alumni at the Ohio State University who are making an effort to improve the web. We work hard to make ourselves, and others, better developers.

Buckeye Hackers

Mondays at 7:00 PM, Caldwell 120

All things hack at OSU. Buckeye Hackers works to create a hacking community at Ohio State. We have weekly coding challenges, news review, and host Ohio State's annual hackathon.

ACM-W

Tuesdays at 12:30 PM, Dreese 159

Engage women in activities and projects that aim to improve the working and learning environments for women in computing by mentoring and rolemodeling.

Electronics Club

Weekly lab hours, Caldwell 078

The hardware hacking club of OSU. The goto place for anything intersting involving electronics.
