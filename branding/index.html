Usage Guidelines

The following are guidelines we've set forth to outline appropriate and innappropriate uses of our marks and branding. We reserve the right to change these at any time, as well as deny any usage that may appear acceptable below.

Yes!

You may use our marks for the following:

  • ▸ To mark your project (website, application, etc.) as having been created at an OHI/O event.
  • ▸ To illustrate OHI/O for discussion in articles (news, research papers, etc.).
  • ▸ To place as a watermark on images taken at OHI/O events.

No!

You may not use our marks for the following:

  • ▸ To imply OHI/O's endorsement of your product or project without explicit permission from us.
  • ▸ To print off your own OHI/O-themed merchandise.
    • ▸ Have a sweet swag idea?
  • ▸ To distribute on your own.
