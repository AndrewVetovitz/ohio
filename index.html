Welcome to Columbus

On November 19th, join us at Ohio State University's largest hackathon for a weekend of building and creating real projects with awesome people. Over 700 undergraduate and graduate students will design, build, and demonstrate software to a live audience of students, faculty, and representatives from tech companies.



Meet other passionate, fun and creative students from OSU and universities across the nation. Our industry partners are also sending professional developers, designers, marketers, and hustlers to provide mentorship.


Work in teams.


Learn something new.


Build something amazing.

HackOHI/O is put on by OHI/O, a student-led organization at Ohio State that aims to foster a tech culture at our university. We're currently planning for the event. Registration will open after the beginning of the fall semester. If you'd like to join our mailing list and be notified of when registration opens, join our mailing list.
